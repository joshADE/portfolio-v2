Grade Tracker

Description

This is a productivity app that tracks a students GPA and CGPA on a term basis. The user can enter in their course details such course code, name, grade, and credit. Once the user enters their details for all their courses, they can select their school on the settings page to use their school's GPA conversion chart. If their school is not in the list of available schools, they can choose 'custom' to allow them to enter their schools grades manually. After their school has been selected they will be able to calcualte their GPA near the bottom of the home page. The inspiration for this app came from another app that my brother told me about called WhatsMyGPA.ca where a user can enter their course grades and credits and have their GPA calculated. To make Grade Tracker unique, I added a few extra features such as being able to move courses around, find courses that meet a certain grade, and keep track of prerequisite as well as corequisites.

Challenges

Grade Tracker was the first project I made using React so it was a bit difficult getting used to JSX syntax as well as concepts such as lifting state, lifecycle methods, etc. I also used Redux for the first time as well which helped solve a lot of problems related to prop drilling. Another big challenge was implementing the logic for editing the corequisites courses which required the use of the Javascript Set class as well as finding the union of two sets.
