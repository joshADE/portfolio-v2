Habitly Landing Page

Description

This project is a landing page that I build for a fictional web/mobile application. The application, called Habitly, tracks health related habits and offers other features such as generating reports, and providing ai based suggestions. I designed the app as part of a challenge for a job. The challenge was to provide an innovative product in Health Industry. For the challenge we (the participants) had to describe our product and many other details such as the product market fit, how we were going to deliver it and target audience. We didn't have time in the challenge to actually develop ours products and I still had mock-ups for the app so I decided it would be nice instead to create a landing page for it.
